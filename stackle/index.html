Welcome to Stackle!

Click and drag to rotate!

Guess the word for each of the 4 faces of the Stackle.

  • Each guess must be a 5-letter word.
  • The first and last letter of each word wraps to adjacent faces, so guess wisely!
  • The color of the tiles will reflect how close your guess was to a word.

Colors

WEARY - W is in the word and in the correct spot.

IILLS - I is in the word but in the wrong spot.

VAGUE - U is in the word of a different face.

GUESS - G is not in the word in any spot.
